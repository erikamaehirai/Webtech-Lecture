What is the meaning of Internet?

Internet is the short term for "Internetwork". It is also defined as the "Network of Networks". The Internet connects millions of people around the
	world and is used mostly for communication and accessing information. Every person that is using the internet has a unique Internet Protocol(IP) address 
	- unique numbers that are used to identify each computer that is connected to the Internet Protocol that are communicating in a network.

	Versions of IP Address
	1. IPv4 - 32 bits long
	2. IPv6 - 128 bits long
